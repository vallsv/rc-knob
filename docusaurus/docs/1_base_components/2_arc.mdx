---
title: Arc
description: Arc component
---

The `Arc` component displays 2 angular zones.

The foreground one which indicates the current knob value, from the `min` to
the knob value.

The background one is from the knob value to `max`.

```jsx live
<Knob size={100} angleOffset={220} angleRange={280} min={0} max={100}>
    <Arc arcWidth={5} color="#FC5A96" background="#CCCCCC" />
</Knob>
```

## Properties

<table>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <th>Prop</th>
            <th>Description</th>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>`arcWidth`</td>
            <td>Width of the arc. The arc will expand to the centre.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>`background`</td>
            <td>
                Color of the arc over the whole range of the knob. If
                `background` is not set, the background arc will not rendered.
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>`color`</td>
            <td>Color of the arc that indicates the value of the knob.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>`radius`</td>
            <td>Outer radius of the arc. Will be the knob `size` by default</td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
</table>
